Welcome to the best way to access golf around NYC!


Here's how it works:


1. Create your profile - it takes less than 30 seconds!

2. Check out some of the available shuttle offerings. These include pre-set routes from CourseCaddy
that are optimized for your convenience and savings, but it also includes ad-hoc requests from other users.
If you'd like to submit a request for a non pre-set route, you can do that too!

3. Sign up for any shuttles you'd like to reserve a spot on! In order to keep costs low for our customers,
we will only run the shuttle if there are a minimum of 4 people signed up.

4. Check out the social portion of the site and see other users on the platform! You can use the built-in
messaging feature to set up games and coordinate playing with new people, or just encourage them to sign
on for certain shuttles. The more people on the same page, the more you save!


Got any questions or feedback? Feel free to reach out to us at CourseCaddy@gmail.com!
